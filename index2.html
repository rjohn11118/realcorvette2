About Us

Welcome to Crusin' Customs! We are a dedicated team of auto mechanics who specialize in the care and repair of Corvettes. With years of experience under our belts, we understand the passion Corvette owners have for their vehicles, and we're here to help you keep your car in perfect condition. Our certified mechanics are trained and updated annually through the NRVA and CRSV programs, ensuring that they have the latest knowledge to service your Corvette. Whether it's a routine maintenance check or a high-performance upgrade, we have the expertise to get the job done right.


At Crusin' Customs, we pride ourselves on fast service—guaranteeing a wait time of less than 2 weeks. If for any reason your service takes longer, we offer lifetime service at no extra charge. We treat every car like it's our own, with top-quality care and attention to detail. We’re more than just mechanics; we’re Corvette enthusiasts dedicated to giving your car the best care possible. Thank you for choosing us as your go-to auto repair shop!
